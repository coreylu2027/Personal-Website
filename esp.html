Intermediate Spanish

In Intermediate Spanish with Sra. Wildfong, we build our language skills by using authentic materials like literature, music, films, games, and class discussions. Instead of learning grammar in isolation, we practice it in real contexts through project-based work such as making podcasts, creating short films, and collaborating in small groups. Our progress is measured through video, audio, and written portfolios that show how our Spanish improves over time.
